Our Heritage & Values

Every scoop and sip reflects our rich heritage of quality, purity, and unmatched flavour. Loved for our iconic Old Vanilla, indulgent Badam Shake, and creamy falooda blends, we bring the authentic taste of India's dessert legacy to your plate.

What Makes Us Special:

  • No artificial cream or frozen desserts
  • Only real milk, nuts, and authentic ingredients
  • Signature classics + seasonal specials
  • Warm service, clean ambience, and rich flavours

Founded by Babulal Dagaji Chaudhari in 1968, Babulal Icecream is a legacy of trust and taste. Our main branch is rooted in Warora, and now we delight Nagpur's ice cream lovers too!
