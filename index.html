Our Story

A Legacy of 58+ Years

Founded in 1968 by Babulal Dagaji Chaudhari, Babulal Icecream began with a simple mission: to deliver premium quality milk products and ice cream that brings joy to every customer.

The business has been lovingly expanded by his three sons - Ramesh, Mahesh, and Shashikant Babulal Chaudhari - who continue the family tradition of excellence, innovation, and pure love in every scoop.
